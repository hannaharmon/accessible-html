Web Accessibility Basics

By Jensyn

Why Accessibility?

There are users with different abilities, devices, and backgrounds. Making websites accessible ensures everyone can use and understand content.

How Accessibility?

A common guideline for accessible web pages is the POUR principle:

  • Perceivable: Information must be presented in ways users can perceive.
  • Operable: Interface components must be usable with different inputs.
  • Understandable: Content must be clear and predictable.
  • Robust: Content must work with assistive technologies.
